About me

Joshua Booth

Hello there! I'm Joshua Booth, a passionate computer science student specializing in software engineering for games development and currently pursuing my master's degree at the University of Hull. My latest project is a collaborative development project as part of my master's degree. In collaboration with ORE Catapult, a leading player in renewable energy innovation, my team and I are creating a VR training software using Unity for the PICO Neo 3. This project is more than just coding; it's a real-world immersion into commercial development practices. From implementing AGILE and PRINCE2 project management techniques to engaging in regular communication and progress updates with our client, we are blending academics with practical industry experience. During this project I have had the opportunity to collaborate with industry experts from Lampada Digital Solutions Limited gaining valuable insight from their industry experience.
